01
Plan in TARN

Crew up. Drop stops. Vote it in.

One shareable link gets your friends in — no signup, no friction. Stops drop on the map. Votes settle the date. Lock it when everyone’s ready. The plan is the plan.

02
Brief in HQ

40+ live data points. One synthesized brief.

The moment TARN locks, HQ pulls live data from NPS, NOAA, USGS, NIFC, Recreation.gov, OpenWeather. Conditions, fire, roads, permits, water, hazards — synthesized into one brief your crew can trust.
